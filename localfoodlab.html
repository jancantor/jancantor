Portfolios for professionals and businesses in the good food movement.

To reflect their attitude, we cut through the unnecessary elements and went with a light, soft but always professional color palette. That way, we could make the photos stand out, and assure people that these people are really there to help you.

The result was a dynamic, totally responsive showcase of every service they have to offer along with a blog to be able to share a bit of their knowledge with the readers.
