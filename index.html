About

I’m a digital designer living in Brooklyn, New York. Currently, I’m a Product Designer at NewsCred where I work with incredibly smart engineers and thinkers who are building a platform for people who love content.

I’m a life-long learner, self-taught, with a passion for crafting beautiful interface and meaningful user experience for the web. Aside from nerding out with pixels, I always have fun playing a couple of ping-pong challenges with friends and likely hunting for the best ramen spot. Otherwise, I like to just lounge and watch Netflix with my fiance and my two furry friends on a lazy weekend.
